【MOBILE SUIT IRON-BLOODED ORPHANS】


There was once a great conflict known as the "Calamity War." Roughly 300 years have passed since the end of this war. The Earth Sphere had lost its previous governing structure, and a new world was created under new systems of government. While a temporary peace had arrived, the seeds of a new conflict were being sown in the Mars Sphere, far away from Earth. Our hero, a boy named Mikazuki Augus, belongs to a private security company called Chryse Guard Security (CGS). The company accepts a mission to escort Kudelia Aina Bernstein, a girl who seeks to liberate the Martian city of Chryse from the rule of one of Earth's major powers. However, the military organization Gjallarhorn attacks CGS in order to nip this rebellion in the bud. CGS begins an evacuation, using Mikazuki and the other children as decoys. Orga Itsuka, the leader of the boys, decides to take this opportunity to rise up in revolt and launch a coup d'état against the adults who have been oppressing them. He gives Mikazuki the task of repulsing Gjallarhorn, and Mikazuki enters battle using the Gundam Barbatos, a mobile suit from the Calamity War era which has been serving as CGS's power source.

【MIKAZUKI AUGUST】


A boy who belongs to Tekkadan. During a previous battle with Gjallarhorn, he overused the Alaya-Vijnana system, and consequently he has lost all sensation in his right eye and hand. However, these sensations can be restored by connecting to the Alaya-Vijnana system, and so he has continued fighting as the pilot of the Gundam Barbatos Lupus

【ORGA ITSUKA】


The leader of Tekkadan. He succeeded in earning overnight fame for Tekkadan during the fighting that surrounded the Arbrau representative election. Thanks to Tekkadan’s accomplishments, the organization has greatly expanded. But its family of members have not stopped working to protect the future, and they are determined to keep moving forward.

【NOBRA SHINO】


Captain of Tekkadan’s First Working Unit. He gives the impression of being a joker, but cares very much about his comrades. He also serves as a drill instructor for new recruits.

【AKIHIRO ALTLAND】


Captain of Tekkadan’s Second Working Unit. He has a well-trained body, and specializes in tasks such as defensive combat and long-duration mobile suit operation.

【VIDAR】


A soldier assigned to the Arianrhod Fleet. He pilots a mobile suit with the same name.

【BARBATOS LUPUS REX】


The Tekkadan Gundam used by Mikazuki Augus. It has taken on a new form after fighting many battles and being upgraded with Teiwaz technology. All its weapons have also been enhanced. This is one of the Gundam Frames used in the Calamity War about 300 years earlier.

【HOTARUBI】


An armed transport belonging to Tekkadan. It was originally a cargo transporter, but since the organization now has so many mobile suits, a dedicated hangar was added, enabling it to carry a large number of mobile suits.

【GUSION REBAKE FULL CITY】


Like the Barbatos, the Gusion Rebake has also been upgraded by Teiwaz into a new form after sustaining severe damage. It retains its previous gimmicks of high-sensitivity camera sensors in the head for long-range precision fire and a “face open” mode for close-range use, and new armament has also been added, including weapons more specialized for close combat.

【RYUSEI-GO】


A Shiden that has been customized for Shino’s personal use. At his request, thrusters have been added to the shoulders and it is equipped with a short shield. It is designed so that Shino can make use of its high mobility in battle. The cockpit seat and other parts have also been transplanted from the old Ryusei-go, making this the only Shiden that can use the Alaya-Vijnana system.

【GUNDAM VIDAR】


A machine that has been undergoing extended maintenance and adjustment by the Arianrhod Fleet. Its true nature, and its systems in particular, are top secret and the details are known only to Rustal and a few others in the fleet.
